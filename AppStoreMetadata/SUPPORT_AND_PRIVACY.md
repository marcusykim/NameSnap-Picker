## Privacy Policy
NameSnap is designed to be privacy-first.

- No account required
- No analytics tracking
- No third-party ads
- No data sold or shared
- All app data stays local on your device

### Data We Collect
None.

### Data Stored On Device
NameSnap may store the following locally on your device to provide app functionality:
- Entered participant names
- Winner history
- App preferences (such as no-repeat mode)

This data is never transmitted to external servers by the app.

### Children’s Privacy
NameSnap does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

If this policy changes in future releases, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
